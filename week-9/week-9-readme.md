## TASK-9 commands

- Route 53 configuration:

- To check DNS propagation:
    - `aws route53 list-resource-record-sets --hosted-zone-id Z3LHP8UIUC8CDK | jq '.ResourceRecordSets[] | select(.Name == "www.denis-bukva.awsbosnia.com.") | {Name, Value}'`
